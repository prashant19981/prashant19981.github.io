Quantitative Trading Analyzer

I developed a comprehensive trading strategy analysis tool that utilizes data from over 200 stocks obtained through the Fyers API using Python. The tool uses various time frames, including 5 minutes, 15 minutes, and daily intervals. To enhance data analysis, I employed the powerful numpy library for data formatting and analysis. Users can input their desired date range to test their trading strategies effectively. The tool incorporates both trend-following and mean reversion strategies. Additionally, I implemented a module that handles trade entry, exit, stop-loss placement, and profit/loss calculations, to get an accurate backtest result. The system also dynamically calculates position size based on risk per trade. For performance evaluation, the tool calculates and displays important metrics such as total profit, maximum drawdown, day-wise and trade-wise accuracy . Furthermore, I utilized the matplotlib library to generate visually appealing graphs of profit and loss over time. With this tool, traders can analyze the profitability and effectiveness of their strategies, enabling them to make informed trading decisions.
